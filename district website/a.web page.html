P.Prasanthi

P.PRASANTHI(I.A.S.-2014)

Collector and District Magistrate

P. Prasanthi is a 2014-batch Indian Administrative Service (IAS) officer from the Andhra Pradesh cadre. She has held several significant administrative positions in the state, demonstrating her versatility and commitment to public service.


Career Highlights

  • West Godavari District Collector: P. Prasanthi served as the inaugural Collector of West Godavari district, headquartered in Bhimavaram. Her tenure was marked by efforts to improve local governance and development initiatives.

  • Director of Agriculture Marketing: In February 2024, she was appointed as the Director of the state's Agriculture Marketing Department. However, her tenure was brief, as she was transferred in the same year.

  • East Godavari District Collector: Following a major bureaucratic reshuffle in June 2024, P. Prasanthi was appointed as the Collector and District Magistrate of East Godavari district, succeeding K. Madhavi Latha.

  • Anantapur Municipal Commissioner: Earlier in her career, she served as the Municipal Commissioner of Anantapur, overseeing urban development and municipal governance.

Recent Developments

In June 2024, the Andhra Pradesh government initiated a significant reshuffle of IAS officers, transferring 18 officials across various districts. As part of this reshuffle, P. Prasanthi was appointed as the Collector and District Magistrate of East Godavari district, a position she currently holds.

Droupadi Murmu

President of India

Droupadi Murmu is the 15th and current President of India, having assumed office on July 25, 2022. She is the first person from a tribal community to hold this esteemed position and the second woman after Pratibha Patil to become President. At 64 years old, she is also the youngest individual to occupy the office and the first to be born after India‚Äôs independence.
