Tourism

East Godavari district in Andhra Pradesh offers diverse tourist attractions, including wildlife sanctuaries, beaches, temples, and historical sites. Key destinations include Coringa Wildlife Sanctuary, Antervedi Beach, Draksharama Temple, and Maredumilli. The region is also known for its scenic beauty along the Godavari River and Konaseema, with opportunities for boat trips and eco-tourism.
